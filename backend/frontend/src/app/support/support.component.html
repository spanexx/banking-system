Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

How do I transfer money?

Navigate to the Transfers section, select your source account, enter the recipient's details, and follow the verification process.

Is online banking secure?

Yes, we use state-of-the-art encryption and security measures to protect your information and transactions.

How do I report suspicious activity?

Contact our 24/7 security hotline immediately at 1-800-SECURE-BANK or email security@simplibank.com
